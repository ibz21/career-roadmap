\# Lab 01 - Basic Navigation



\## Goal

Practice moving around the terminal and understanding where I am.



\## Commands used

\- cd

\- dir

\- pwd



\## What I practised

\- moving into a folder

\- moving back up with ..

\- checking my current location

\- listing what is inside a folder

\- creating files and folders from the terminal



\## Why this matters

These are the first building blocks of terminal confidence.
